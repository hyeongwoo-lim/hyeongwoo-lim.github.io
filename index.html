I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at POSTECH's Future Fluid and Biomimetics Laboratory. My research centers on interfacial transport phenomena, functional porous materials, and solar-driven seawater desalination. Building on this foundation, I aim to extend my thermofluidic expertise toward broader thermal management, resource recovery, and waste material/energy reuse technologies.
